ShapeFight — Data Deletion

Developer: AUgames (Aziz Üzümcü) · App: ShapeFight on Google Play (package com.shapefight)

What data ShapeFight handles

ShapeFight is a free-to-play offline game with no user accounts and no servers operated by AUgames. The only data the app handles:

  • Local save file on your device — your in-game gold, upgrades, unlocked units, checkpoint progress, and selected language. Stored only on your phone or tablet. AUgames has no copies.
  • AdMob Advertising ID — collected by Google AdMob when the app shows a rewarded video ad. Tied to your device, resettable at any time, not stored by AUgames.

How to delete your data

1. Delete the local save file

  1. Open your device's Settings
  2. Go to Apps (or Application Manager)
  3. Find and tap ShapeFight
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear data (or Clear storage)

This wipes your save file. Your gold, upgrades, and progress are permanently removed. The app reopens to a fresh first-launch state.

2. Or simply uninstall the app

Uninstalling ShapeFight (long-press the icon → Uninstall, or via Play Store) deletes everything including the save file. There is nothing to clean up afterwards.

3. Reset or limit ad tracking (optional)

Independently of ShapeFight, you can reset Google's Advertising ID at any time:

  1. Settings → Google → Ads
  2. Tap Reset advertising ID (or Delete advertising ID on newer Android versions)

This breaks the link between your device's advertising history and any ad networks, including AdMob.

What's deleted vs retained

  • Deleted immediately: Your local save file (the only data AUgames' app creates).
  • Retained by Google: Any data Google AdMob collected during ad serving is retained according to Google's own privacy policy. Resetting your Advertising ID prevents future ad-network linkage.
  • AUgames servers: AUgames operates no servers and stores no data. There is nothing on our side to delete.
