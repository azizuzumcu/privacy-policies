ShapeFight — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-07 · Effective for ShapeFight v1.1.0 and later.

This privacy policy explains what information the ShapeFight Android app ("ShapeFight", "the game", "we") collects, how it is used, and the third parties involved. ShapeFight is developed and published by AUgames, a solo Turkish indie game studio.

1. Information ShapeFight collects directly

None. ShapeFight has no user accounts, no login, no online features, and no servers operated by AUgames. All gameplay progress (gold, unlocked units, upgrade levels, selected language, checkpoint progress) is saved locally on your device only.

2. Information collected by third-party services

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

ShapeFight displays rewarded video ads when you tap the "Watch Ad" button on the defeat screen to continue playing. We use Google AdMob for these ads. AdMob may collect the following:

  • Advertising ID (also called AAID) — a resettable per-device identifier
  • Coarse device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Interaction with ads (impressions, clicks)

ShapeFight requests the com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID Android permission to enable AdMob to read the Advertising ID. You can reset or opt out of the Advertising ID at any time from your device's system settings (typically under Privacy or Google → Ads).

AdMob's own privacy practices are described in Google's Privacy Policy and the AdMob privacy guidance.

3. Information ShapeFight does NOT collect

  • No email, name, phone number, or any account credentials
  • No precise GPS location
  • No contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera access
  • No analytics SDK (no Firebase, no Crashlytics, no Flurry)
  • No in-app purchases — ShapeFight is free-to-play, ad-supported only

4. Data retention

Your local save file (gold, upgrades, language preference) lives on your device only and is deleted when you uninstall the app. AUgames retains no copies. Any data AdMob collects is retained according to Google's own retention policies, linked above.

5. Children's privacy

ShapeFight is suitable for general audiences. The user interface text uses simple wording that's accessible to children. We do not knowingly target users under 13 with personalized advertising. If your account is configured for a child, Google's ad systems may automatically restrict the ad categories shown.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as ShapeFight evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of ShapeFight after a change means you accept the updated policy.
